Features
--------

Here a list of some intersesting features you can have with Tyrs.

* OAuth authentification.
* UTF-8 support.
* Transarency and colors.
* Based on ncurses.
* Customisation of keys binding.
* Customisation of colors.
* Easely configurable, readable configuration.
* Opens URL just with 'o' touch.
* Tweets, Retweets, Follow, Unfollow.

[IMPORTANT]
This client is relatively new, started current May 2011,  and curently in full development phase, there
are lots of functionnality that will be done soon.
